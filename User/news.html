Dhaka University secured the First and Second positions

At the 2022 International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) Asia Dhaka Regional Contest organised by Green University of Bangladesh (GUB), Dhaka University secured the first and second positions while Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET) came in third. Team DU Kronos – consisting of Yeamin Kaiser, Bholenath Das Niloy, and Sakib Hasan – representing Dhaka University was declared the champion of the competition while team DU Not Ready Yet also representing Dhaka University was announced as the first runner-up. From BUET, team BUET Sommohito secured the second runner-up position. This time, 162 teams from 92 universities across the country participated in the final competition.

CSEDU Ambidexterity Award

CSEDU Ambidexterity Award celebrates excellence in both academic results and programming contest arena. Achieving distinction in both is extremely difficult given the efforts required are humongous. Ayman Rashid, Tasnim Jubaier, and Jubayer Rahman (Nirjhor) are the winners of this prestigious award for the year 2019. Ayman and Tasnim are from the 23rd batch and Jubayer is from the 24th batch of CSEDU. All of them are excellent problem solvers. They are also showing great promise in academic results. CSEDU really appreciates the efforts they are putting in and wishes them the very best in future...

CSEDU Alumnus X Receives NSF Grant for Groundbreaking Research

We are thrilled to announce that Mr X, a graduate of our Computer Science and Engineering program, has been awarded a prestigious grant from the National Science Foundation (NSF) for his groundbreaking research in the field of artificial intelligence. X, who earned his bachelor's degree from our program in 2010 and went on to complete his PhD at a leading research university, is now a tenure-track professor at a major research institution. His work focuses on developing algorithms and techniques for machine learning that can more accurately predict human behavior and decision-making.
