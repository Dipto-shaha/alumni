Who We Are?

CSEDU Alumni Association is a non profit alumni association of the students of CSE Department of the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh. CSEDUAA was founded at February 6, 2009.The CSEDUAA is guided by its Constitution in its organizational structure as well as its management system.The Constitution of CSEDU Alumni gives, among many other important information, details of the rules and regulations of how the BOARD OF TRUSTEES(BOT) and EXECUTIVE COMMITTEEEC are formed and the responsibilities that each member of them bears



Welcome to CSEDU Alumni Association

Our mission

To create a purposeful and forward-looking organization for the alumni of CSEDU for their social, cultural, and professional pursuits; for fostering greater cohesion and fellowship among themselves; for rendering all possible support to the transformation of the beloved alma mater into an institution of higher learning in technical and science education that is among the best in the world; and for contributing to accelerated and sustainable national development.

Our objectives

  • Develop stronger bond and cohesion among the members and well-wishers of the Association.
  • Enhance and uphold professional competency and integrity of the members
  • Appropriately link, liaise, and complement the activities of the various societies or clubs of CSEDU graduates that may exist in Bangladesh and in foreign countries.
  • Provide support to the members and their families as well CSEDU students/staff members in distress and to organize humanitarian activities in times of national calamities
  • Develop and maintain a database of all members, which would include information on areas of their special expertise and interest, and maintaining regular communication with them through mails, publications, and web page that would facilitate their participation in the activities of the Association
  • Arrange social activities such as reunions, picnics, dinners, cultural programs, and exchange visits for greater social cohesion among members.
